Founded in 1989, 94 local groups, 32 countries : discover BEST !

BEST (Board of European Students of Technology) is a voluntary, apolitical, non-profit and non-representative international association of European students of technology. Since 1989, we fight to rise the interest of students in technology towards foreign countries, by encouraging mobility and intercultural exchanges.

Empowering diversity

People understand and respect different cultures and societies. An environment of empowered diversity supports people in to apply their full potential and to act responsibly.

Developing students

BEST helps students to achieve an international mindset, to reach a better understanding of cultures and societies and to develop the capacity to work in culturally diverse environments. BEST creates opportunities for the personal development of students and supports them in reaching their full potential.

  • Flexibility : We seek the ability to make changes and to deal with changing conditions. We are open to everything new. We value and nurture the ability of being mobile and to quickly respond to changes in the environment or to any other obstacle that we face.

  • Friendship : We build good relationships where people help, support and care for one another. We value good personal relations and teamwork. We focus on each person involved in our activities and in this way we create synergy.

  • Fun : We enjoy everything we do. We value the positive emotions of the people involved in our actions. We strive to make our activities enjoyable to everybody who is connected to them. We act with passion and strive to share this passion with the people around us.

  • Improvement : We strive to continuously improve the standards of everything we do. We use all our creativity to enhance the way we work. This is to guarantee the constant development of our actions.

  • Learning : We gain skills and understanding through experience. We value everything we learn through the experience of being involved in our activities and we strive to learn as much as possible from every aspect of our work. We value personal development and strive for an open learning community where we have the freedom to share and develop ideas.

BCS (BEST Career Support) gathers the services proposed by BEST for students to navigate more easily in their career. We are looking for unique profesional opportunities for students from technological universities in Europe so that they can begin an international career or study abroad. BEST informs students about those international paths, to learn through travels and mobility. You can access our offers using our Career Support tools : BEST international website, BEST database, career newsletter, social networks (facebook, twitter, linkedin) ...
Follow these few steps to access opportunities :

  1. Create a profile in BEST international website
  2. Fill in your profile : picture, CV, education ...
  3. Like BEST and follow us on social networks to hear about our application periods
  4. Candidate for BEST Career Day
  5. Enjoy your new career ! Use it to develop yourself, learn plenty of tips and do some networking all around Europe

Interested in helping us ? Join BEST !

Every students registered at INSA Lyon can be a member of BEST Lyon.

You give to BEST as much time as you decide, out of your own free will. Some members are more active and available than the others, and everyone gets to choose what he/she wants to do in BEST. By meeting us you will get to discover the large opportunities offered to you by BEST, independently of your study domain.

As a member of BEST, you will get the opportunity to :

  • Organize innovative events targeting european students
  • Get trainings on several skills (time management, project management, fundraising, marketing, IT ...) throughout the year, both locally and internationally
  • Participate in international internal events (Regional Meeting, General Assembly ...) where students from everywhere around Europe gather
  • Participate in international projects managed by the international departments of BEST
  • Participate in our local internal events, such as motivational week-ends, meetings and leisure activities

You can join us everyday Monday at 20:00 at Marie-Curie library or contact us here

BEST brings students, companies and universities together.

BEST gathers students and companies. We attract the students who will later become the next generation of scientists and engineers. Students are looking for companies, companies need employees : a partnership between BEST and your company constitutes an asset that proved itself everywhere around Europe. Jump in !

BEST Lyon organizes and facilitates events gathering INSA engineers or universities belonging to our european network. As a partner of our association, you benefit of many opportunities :

  • Get to meet today the engineers and managers of tomorrow
  • Get a foot in the door of la Doua, the biggest technological campus in France
  • Benefit of the dynamic image of BEST to highlight your company activities by becoming sponsor of our events
  • Become a part of our large network involving 94 prestigious universities and more than 1,000,000 students
  • Present your company during seasonal events and/or conferences

BEST wants to establish strong bonds between students, companies and universities. Our goal is to facilitate the creation of these relationships. Owing to this point, we propose several services such as your logo on our marketing material, participation or proposition of a case study, organization of a conference, involvement in our events ...
Don't hesitate to contact us so that we can adjust our offer to your needs and best interest !
